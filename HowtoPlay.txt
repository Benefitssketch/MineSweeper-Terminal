This won't be of much help if you don't already understand how to play minesweeper. For an explanation on that, check out: www.minesweepergame.com/strategy/how-to-play-minesweeper.php

Usage guide for this program:
Based on the game mode you select, something like this should be printed on-screen:

     A|B|C|D|E|F|G|H|I

 1   * * * * * * * * *
 2   * * * * * * * * *
 3   * * * * * * * * *
 4   * * * * * * * * *
 5   * * * * * * * * *
 6   * * * * * * * * *
 7   * * * * * * * * *
 8   * * * * * * * * *
 9   * * * * * * * * *

This is the board where the game is played. Just like the classic game your first step is to select a random space to start the game. How does selecting spaces work? I'm glad you asked!

First you'll be prompted to enter a row. You can select from any of the numbers on the left side of the screen (in this case the options are 1-9).
Afterwards, you'll be prompted to enter a column corresponding to the letters at the top of the screen (in this case A-I).

For your first move this will reveal a number of squares to get the game started.

On subsequent turns, there are three possible actions you can input:
0 - If you change your mind, this will cancel your selections and set you back to the row selection step.
1 - Reveal the selected space in classic Minesweeper fashion. But if it's a mine, you lose!
2 - Place a flag on the selected space.
